# 🏫 HfWU Intercampus Mobility — Corporate Mobility Management

A Corporate Mobility Management (CMM) concept for Nürtingen-Geislingen University (NGU/HfWU), proposing a low-emission intercampus shuttle service by repurposing existing university-owned minibuses.

**Course:** SUM 2 II.4 — Mobility Policies 1  
**University:** HfWU Nürtingen-Geislingen  
**Date:** June 2025

---

## 📋 Overview

HfWU operates two campuses approximately **40 km apart** (Nürtingen and Geislingen). Staff and students frequently commute between campuses for shared lectures, but the trip is dominated by **private car use**, contributing to the university's greenhouse gas emissions.

The university owns several minibuses that are only used sporadically for excursions. This project proposes restructuring these vehicles into a **coordinated intercampus shuttle** aligned with academic schedules.

### Research Question
> *How can Corporate Mobility Management be applied at NGU to develop a low-emission intercampus travel solution by repurposing existing university-owned minibuses?*

---

## 🔑 Key Components

- **Problem Analysis:** High car dependency, underutilized vehicle fleet, CO₂ reduction goals
- **Best Practice Case:** Hochschule Neu-Ulm (HNU) — employee shuttle program
- **Theoretical Framework:** Corporate Mobility Management, Sustainable Mobility Paradigm
- **Proposed Solution:** Scheduled shuttle service synced with joint academic activities
- **Impact:** Reduced emissions, improved resource efficiency, enhanced campus connectivity
